# Cover Letter Template

This template is used as a reference for AI-generated cover letters.

## Structure

### Opening (1 paragraph)
- Express genuine interest in the specific role
- Mention where you found the position
- Brief hook connecting your experience to their needs

### Body (2 paragraphs)

**Paragraph 1 - Relevant Experience:**
- Highlight most relevant past role(s)
- Quantify achievements where possible
- Connect directly to job requirements

**Paragraph 2 - Skills & Value:**
- Technical skills that match requirements
- Soft skills with concrete examples
- What you'll bring to the organization

### Closing (1 paragraph)
- Reiterate enthusiasm
- Mention availability
- Clear call to action

## Things to Avoid

- Generic openings ("I am writing to apply...")
- Repeating the CV verbatim
- Focusing only on what you want (vs. what you offer)
- Typos and grammatical errors
- Being too long (keep under 400 words)
